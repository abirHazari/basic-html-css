Grilled Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple Skewers

Description

These savory-sweet shrimp skewers are easy to make and cook in just a few minutes. They are perfect for summer weeknights or weekend dinner parties. Prep the sauce ahead of time for an even faster dinner.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Shrimp
  • Pineapple
  • Cilantro
  • Sesame Seeds

Steps

  1. Combine water,soy sauce,brown sugar, honey, garlic and ginger in a saucepan
  2. Wait until it has reduced and thickened slightly
  3. Thread the shrimp and pineapple in a skewer and grill then until brown
  4. Then brush the sauce on both side of the skewers and sprinkle with cilantro and sesame seed
  5. Serve!
