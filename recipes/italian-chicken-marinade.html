Italian Chicken Marinade

description

This Italian dressing chicken marinade is a super simple but delicious way to add flavor before grilling.

Ingredients

  • Salad dressing
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chicken
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk salad dressing,garlic powder,salt and pepper together
  2. Coat the chicken with the mixture and marinade for 4 hours
  3. Shake of the excess marinade and grill the chicken until crispy
  4. Serve!
